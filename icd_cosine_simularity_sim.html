ICD Code Repair Simulator with Cosine Similarity Analysis

This simulator demonstrates a cosine similarity analysis of patient notes. The note is broken into individual lines, and for each line the simulator computes similarity scores against a repository of candidate ICD codes and their descriptions. The overall scores for each candidate are then tallied, simulating how one might compare individual aspects of the note with the overall content to suggest the best ICD code.
